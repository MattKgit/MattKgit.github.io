VR Crane Simulator
Packet39

As part of XR Terra AR & VR Developer Certificate Program, we had the opportunity to work with a proven high-tech solution provider Packet39. Given the problem of creating a crane simulator in VR and collecting relevant performance measurements, we needed to model the physics of the crane, create controls for an operator seated in the cab, and map use of the controls to movements of the crane in VR.

(Description of functionality needed.)

Achievements

  • Team Lead. Led daily meetings with outlined agendas.
  • Broke down functionality to implement.
  • Helped initially shape crucial content and structure in the design document.
  • Helped prepare critical questions for the client
  • Worked out the 3D physics in Unity to replicate controls and consequential movements of a crane.
  • The crane was able to pick up a load and transport it to a raised location.
  • Performance metrics on successful placement, and time taken to complete the task where maintained and presented to the user at the end of the task.
  • Created controls to rotate the cab left or right, raise or lower the boom, extend or retract the sub boom, and lower and raise the hook.
  • There was a button acting like an ignition which needed to be pressed to start and end the activity, and enable the controls.
  • There was a button which served as a magnet, on the hook, to pick up and release the load.
  • Helped integrate setting materials
  • Helped to integrate sound effects accompanying button pressing, as well as for each of the four level controls.
  • Displayed instructions to the user which could be referenced by looking up.
  • Worked on hand posture and movement to activate lever in VR.
