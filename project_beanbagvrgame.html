Bean Bag VR Game
XR Terra AR & VR Developer Program

As part of XR Terra AR & VR Developer Certificate Program, we were given the assignment to create a VR Bean Bag Toss game, replete with functionality that kept score for two players.

(Description of functionality needed.)

Achievements

  • I created a two-player VR game (using single Oculus Quest headset).
  • The player uses distance grabbing to pick up a bean bag and toss it/drop it on a cornhole game board.
  • Implemented a scoring system, rounds, win condition, and ability to play again.
  • Created a "debug" window to help with debugging issues in VR
  • Played soothing background music, and included sound effects when the bean bags hit the board, the ground, and went through the hole.
